Chicken and Noodles



Ingredients
For the Noodles:
Flour 2 cups
Salt 1 Teaspoon
Egg Yolks 3 Egg Yolks
Whole Egg 1 Egg
Water 1/2 cup
Extra Flour For Kneading
Everything else:
Chicken 3-4 Large Chicken Breasts
Chicken Buillon 6 Teaspoons
Water 4 1/2 Quarts (Divided)
Directions
  1. Put 2 cups of water in an InstantPot. Place chicken breasts in InstantPot and pressure cook for 8 minutes if thawed, or 15 minutes if frozen. Once done let cool and shred chicken. Set aside.
  2. For the noodles:
    • Put remaining 4 quarts of water into large saucepan and let boil.
    • In a medium bowl- mix flour, salt, egg yolks, and whole egg. Mix together, then add water and mix again. If dough is too sticky add more flour, if dough is too dry add more water.
    • Knead dough on floured surface until no longer sticky and is stretchy.
    • Let dough sit for 10 minutes while covered.
    • Divide dough into 4 parts. On a floured surface roll one part at a time until around 1/8 inch thick. Fold each part into thirds like a letter and cut narrow strips around 1/4 inch wide.
    • Once all the noodles are cut I like to break the longer pieces so they are shorter. Noodles can be dried and then frozen for later or used immediately.
  3. If the water is already boiling, add the buillion and stir. Once the buillon is dissolved add the shredded chicken.
  4. Add the noodles to boiling water (if not boiling noodles will stick together) and let cook uncovered for 12 minutes. Stir occasionally.
  5. To thicken the gravy, mix a few tablespoons of water and flour together to make a thin paste in seperate cup. Mix in and let look for a minute to see if it has gotten thick enough. If not, repeat until desired thickness.
  6. I usually serve this with mashed potatoes, corn, and crescent rolls. Enjoy!

*This is a great recipe for potlucks or parties! I move it all once it's cooked into a large crockpot on a low setting and leave it on my counter for a few hours. This way my family can just come eat when they're hungry and it's still warm!*
