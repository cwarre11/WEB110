Project Milestone #2

What it's actually like to work at Amazon

Web Site Goal

To tell everyone about working at Amazon. Most articles online are from a journalist who haven't actually worked for Amazon and is just based off what they've heard or possibly what they've made up.

This will be based off of a Fulfillment Center, which is the most common building type. There are also delivery stations but they're way smaller and employ way fewer people.

What my form will be

Ask for opinions from others that have/do work for Amazon. It would help me gather information for delivery stations and from delivery drivers.

Where I'll obtain information

Most of the information will come from my personal experience from working at Amazon. Some will come from Amazon groups I'm currently a part of where we discuss/complain about our jobs.

Pictures used will have to be obtained from online because we normally aren't allowed to have phones at Amazon, so I can't take any pictures myself. Taking a picture inside Amazon without permission could get me fired if I was caught.
